Cartography

Cartography is the art and science of map-making, involving the design, production, and study of maps. Key elements include:
  • Map Design: Creating maps that are visually appealing and easy to understand, using principles of design such as color theory, typography, and layout.
  • Cartographic Techniques: Traditional methods like hand-drawing maps as well as modern digital mapping using software.
  • Data Representation: Using symbols, lines, and colors to represent different data types and terrain features.
